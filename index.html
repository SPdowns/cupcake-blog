Day Two

Our professor today taught the history of 350ºF. They said they always start the semesters with this lesson so as to ensure we start to understand it at the end of the course. 350ºF is the essential tempurature for every cupcake to become their best cake. Starting in 2000 B.C. in ancient Greece when they would produce a nut cake, they new 350ºF was how to make these cakes. Hopefully, we will appriciate this tempurate for all its done so we can enjoy our most precious cupcakes today.
